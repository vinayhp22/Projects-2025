#  Property Rental & Booking Platform (Like Airbnb)

#### Features:
##### Property Listings: Owners can list their properties with images and descriptions.
##### Booking & Availability Calendar: Users can check availability and book stays.
##### Reviews & Ratings: Tenants can rate properties and hosts.
